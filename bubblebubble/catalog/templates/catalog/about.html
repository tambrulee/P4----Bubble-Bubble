Our Story

Vegan Cruelty-free Made in the UK

Moon & Moss was created from a love of simple rituals — the kind that slow you down and bring you back to yourself.

We make small-batch, organic soaps inspired by nature, comfort, and quiet moments. From woodland scents like cedarwood and pine, to soft florals and indulgent blends that feel almost edible, each bar is designed to turn everyday washing into something a little more intentional.

Our soaps are made using thoughtfully sourced ingredients, chosen for both their scent and their purpose. We believe skincare should be gentle, honest, and rooted in nature — without feeling dull or overly worthy. That’s why you’ll find both straightforward, single-note bars and more playful, dessert-inspired blends sitting side by side in our range.

What we care about

  • Small-batch Made in limited runs with care and attention to detail.
  • Organic Thoughtfully chosen ingredients with a gentle, skin-friendly feel.
  • Sensory Scents and textures designed to feel like a quiet moment to yourself.

Moon & Moss is about balance: the wild and the soft, the practical and the indulgent, the everyday and the ritual. Whether it’s the calm of Lavender Moon, the warmth of Honey Cinnamon, or the freshness of Organic Mint, we hope each bar brings a quiet moment of comfort into your day.
