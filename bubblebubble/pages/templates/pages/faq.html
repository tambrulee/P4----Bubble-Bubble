Frequently Asked Questions

Are your soaps suitable for sensitive skin?

Our soaps are made using gentle, natural ingredients. However, we recommend checking ingredient lists carefully and performing a patch test.

Are your products vegan and cruelty-free?

Moon & Moss products are cruelty-free. Any vegan-friendly products will be clearly labelled.

Do you accept custom orders?

At this time, custom orders are not available.

How should I store my soap?

Store your soap in a cool, dry place and allow it to dry fully between uses to extend its lifespan.
